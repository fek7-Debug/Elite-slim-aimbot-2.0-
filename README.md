# 🚀 Elite Slim Hub v23 | Universal & Mobile Optimized

**Elite Slim Hub** is a high-performance, multi-game script hub designed for Roblox. This version (v23) focuses on extreme stability, real-time performance tracking, and a seamless mobile experience.

---

## 🔫 Features

### 🎯 Aimbot & Combat
* **Advanced Aimbot:** Smooth tracking with customizable prediction.
* **Smart FOV:** Fully adjustable Field of View circle (No-fill design).
* **Prediction System:** Hit your shots even when enemies are moving fast.
* **Team & Wall Check:** Professional raycasting to avoid targeting teammates or shooting through walls.

### 👁️ Slim Visuals (ESP)
* **Box ESP:** Clean white outlines around players.
* **Skeleton ESP:** See enemy posture through walls.
* **Health Bar:** Dynamic color-changing bars based on enemy HP.
* **Name & Tracers:** High-visibility text and snaplines from the top.

### ⚡ Performance & Stats
* **Mini Floating Windows:** Draggable real-time FPS and Ping monitors.
* **Ultra FPS Boost:** Removes textures, shadows, and particles for maximum frames.
* **International Support:** Entire interface translated to English.

### 🌐 World Hacks
* **Spin Bot:** Anti-backstab rotation (works in First Person).
* **Fullbright:** Force daylight and remove darkness permanently.
* **Infinite Jump:** Never touch the ground again.
* **Anti-AFK:** Stay connected without getting kicked.
